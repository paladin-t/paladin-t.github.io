How to play Hyper Dungeon Crawler?

Interface:

  • 1. Player's basic information
  • 2. Level information, menu button
  • 3. Craft button
  • 4. Inventory button
  • 5. Handy bar
  • 6. Skip one turn
  • 7. Change action speed

Click to destroy environments.

Click to collect materials.

Craft with collected ingredients.

Select an item in handy bar to equip tools and weapons.

Select an item in handy bar to equip bomb and spells, then throw or cast it in scene.

Click to harvest.

Click to collect food ingredients.

Click to battle.

Click to collect materials dropped from enemies.

Eat to heal yourself.

Find portal to step into next stage.

Now you are ready to compose your own legend.
