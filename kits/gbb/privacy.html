GB BASIC

A retro Fantasy Console that generates ROMs compatible with the popular handheld game console.

Privacy Policy

GB BASIC is a Fantasy Console running on Desktop platforms that supports the use of built-in editors to help you make retro games and run them on devices or emulators.

This privacy policy applies to the main software itself, its embedded frameworks and other built-in features.

Purchases

I have set up the pricing for GB BASIC, see pricing strategy for details.

Data Collected by GB BASIC

In short, the GB BASIC software itself does not collect any data from you. I don't do any data tracking technology for "Growth Hacking" purpose, I don't even count how many users I have. I just want to provide a handy and safe Fantasy Console, and do not care how "success" I am, or how the software is being used by users.

Please be confident about our service, all secrets stay on your device, I don't have control over them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact Me

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me at the message board or get my E-mail address.
