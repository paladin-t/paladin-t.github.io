Urban Player

Get Urban Player

  • Alpha version on Itch

Urban Player is an oldschool music player and visualizer.
When I was in a vehicle thinking about literally nothing, a breeze touching my face felt cool yet warm, and the landscape embracing me brought my mind into a trance. The idea of this app just came from that moment. I created Urban Player to reproduce the feeling digitally. You can treat it as:

  • A music player and visualizer
  • A racing game
  • Or just a light-interaction app which chills you out
  • Moreover, you can also make your own scene for Urban Player

Usage:
  • Music mode. The app has included a few built-in example music. You can pick other mp3, ogg, etc. from your local drive.
  • Racing mode. Z to drive forward, X to break, left/right arrow keys to turn.
