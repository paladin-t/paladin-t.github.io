[col=0x6f95abff]Tips: Operations[/col]

Most operations in TLOP are done by
mouse. In addition, double click on a
citizen or animal to follow them.

Press and hold to upgrade 10 times or
purchase 10 times in the Up. or shop.

TLOP accepts the following keyboard
shortcuts:

* "Ctrl+0" to use mini window
* "Ctrl+1/2/3/4" to use landscape window
* "Ctrl+Shift+1/2/3/4" to use portrait window
* "F10" to take a screenshot
