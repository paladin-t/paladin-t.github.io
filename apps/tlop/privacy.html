The Legend of Pomodoro

A gamified pomodoro tool and an RPG game driven by tomatoes.

Privacy Policy

The Legend of Pomodoro is a Pomodoro tool for Desktop, Mobile and Browser platforms that supports the use of built-in timer and gamified progress to help you be focused on daily tasks.

This privacy policy applies to the main app itself, its embedded frameworks and other built-in features.

Purchases

We have set up the pricing for Basic and Advanced versions of The Legend of Pomodoro respectively, see pricing strategy for details.

The Legend of Pomodoro doesn't gather or use any data of your purchase information.

Data Collected by The Legend of Pomodoro

In short, the The Legend of Pomodoro app itself does not collect any data from you. We don't do any data tracking technology for "Growth Hacking" purpose, we don't even count how many users we have. We just want to provide a handy and safe Pomodoro tool, and do not care how "success" we are, or how the app is being used by users.

Please be confident about our service, all secrets stay on your device, we don't have control over them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the message board or get my E-mail address.
